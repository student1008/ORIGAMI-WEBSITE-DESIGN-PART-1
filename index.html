ORIGAMI- A UNIQUE ARTWORK

What is Origami?

  • Origami is a word formed from 2 ancient Chinese Words.
  • One of the Words being "Oru" (Paper) and "Kami" (Folding)
  • Oru+Kami=Orukami which was later modified to "Origami"

    • Fun Facts:

    • The art was originally known as "Orikata"
    • Fun Fact: An ancient Japanese legend said,"If you can fold 1000 paper crane birds, then you will be granted a wish! "
